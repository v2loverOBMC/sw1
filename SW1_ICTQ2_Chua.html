Bernice's Music Mix

A Collection of My favorite artists, albums, and songs

My Favorite Artists

Tyler the Creator

Eclectic rapper, producer, and designer known for his bold, genre-blending music and unique, often controversial artistic vision. Key member of Odd Future.

Lana del Rey

Singer-songwriter celebrated for her cinematic, melancholic sound, blending pop, indie, and baroque influences with themes of love, heartbreak, and nostalgia.

Mac demarco

Indie rock musician recognized for his laid-back, lo-fi sound, quirky personality, and heartfelt, often humorous lyrics.

Frank Ocean

Influential R&B artist known for his genre-defying music, introspective lyrics, and emotional vulnerability, often exploring love, identity, and self-reflection.

My Favorite Pinoy Artists

Rey Valera

Renowned Filipino singer-songwriter, known for his heartfelt ballads and significant contributions to OPM.

Eva Eugenio

Popular Filipino singer from the '80s, known for her emotive voice and iconic hits in the OPM genre.

My Favorite Bands

The Smiths

English indie rock band from the '80s, led by Morrissey and Johnny Marr, known for melancholic lyrics and jangly guitar sounds.

Silent Sanctuary

Filipino band blending symphonic elements with alternative rock, known for emotional ballads and intricate arrangements.

Eraserheads

Iconic Filipino rock band often called the "Beatles of the Philippines," known for their catchy hits and influence on the OPM scene.

The Itchyworms

Filipino pop-rock band known for their playful sound and relatable lyrics, with a mix of humor and introspection.

Orange & Lemons

Filipino pop band recognized for their retro-inspired sound, catchy melodies, and songs that evoke nostalgia and romance.

Louder Than Bombs
